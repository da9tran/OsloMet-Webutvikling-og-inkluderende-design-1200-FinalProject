GreenRoutine is both an app and a specially designed recycling machine made to both encourage and give people a reason to recycle the garbage and clean up in their communities. By recycling trash, you can earn points.


The points you get is calculated based on the weight of each bag of trash that is put into our special designed garbage box and then sent into the app. The points you earn can be used at various stores to get discounts, and you can compete between friends in your community to recycle and gather the most points.

Our partners:

Co-operation with Norsk Gjenvinning, who will help us sort through the waste that you will recycle.

Co-operation with Oslo Municipality, because of their already existing recycle program.

In co-operation with Fretex, our machines will be found in most neighborhoods.

Our sponsors:

Deals to get cheaper food and items in exchange for recycling garbage.

Narvesen provides discount on food and drinks through the point system.

15 min of free bicycle time for recycling a set amount of garbage.
