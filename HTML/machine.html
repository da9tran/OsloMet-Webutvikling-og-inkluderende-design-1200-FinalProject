The machine operates by performing three main tasks:

The illustration above shows what you will first encounter when you go to one of our machines. Our machine has a built-in screen. Under it is a scanner which will scan your personal QR-code provided on our app. By scanning the QR-code, the hatch on your left will open for you to place your trash bags inside.



Inside of the machine is a hatch, a color-scanning device and a weight. A conveyor belt moves your trash bags to the weight, which will register the weight of your bags into a certain amount of points on your app.



Depicted above is the back-end of our machine. These tubes sort your trash bags into their respective waste container.
